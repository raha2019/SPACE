Mentor Note

Scoring weights and warning thresholds are preliminary and intended for adjustment based on staff/PI feedback, Matterport measurements, SUMS, 3DPrinterOS, and future observation data.

Method

Two-step evaluation: (1) feasibility checks for overlap, egress, and corridor blockage; (2) weighted scoring across seven layout-quality metrics, modulated by traffic and active-use mode.
